The following steps are a quick overview of how Digital Sales Aid is set up by your organization.

Step 1: Administrator Downloads DSA from AppExchange

Your administrator downloads the install package from AppExchange.

Step 2: Administrator Configures Salesforce for DSA

Your administrator uses the Configuration Wizard to configure your Salesforce instance to run the application.

Step 3: Sign in from the iPad Application

Log in to the application using your Salesforce username and password.

Step 4: Wait for Data to Synchronize

Your Salesforce data will automatically synchronize with the application.

You are now ready to use the DSA!
