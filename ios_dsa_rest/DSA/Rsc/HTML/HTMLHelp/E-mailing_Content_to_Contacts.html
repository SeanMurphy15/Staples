Step 1: Send as Button (if applicable)

When viewing any document or image, the Send as E-mail button allows you to forward the item. To send a document or image, tap Send as Email (A) when the item is open.

Note: The Send as E-mail button only shows up if the document type in Salesforce is NOT set to "Competitive Information."

Step 2: Selecting Contacts

Enter a search string (first name, last name, etc.) in the window (B). Matches will appear in the window below. Tap on the name (A) to select it.

Note: the list of contacts is determined by your access in Salesforce. If you have a new contact, just tap the E-mail Selected button and enter the email address in the To: field at the compose screen (see Step 3).

Step 3: Composing the Email Message

An e-mail message will appear populated with the file, the recipient address, the subject information, and the attachment (A). To send the e-mail, click Send (B).
