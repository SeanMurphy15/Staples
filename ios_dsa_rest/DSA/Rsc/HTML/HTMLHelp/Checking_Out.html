Checking Out

To check out a contact, click Check Out. The Email to contact switch allows you to e-mail the item to a prospect, regardless of whether you have tapped the Send as email link during your presentation. (See E-mail Content to Contacts for more information on this feature.) Notes: section (B) section allows you to enter any notes, if desired--just tap the area and the virtual keyboard will appear.
 
When finished, click the Done button on the top right of the screen or Cancel to disregard.
