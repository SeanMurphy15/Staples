Home Screen Settings Menu

Settings Menu, at the top right corner of the screen, allows you to
- Sign In/ Sign Out
- Report a Problem - link to a support page
- Manually Synchronize
- Get to these Help Pages
- Look at the HTML5 Model Cola Example
- About DSA

Searching for Content

The Search window (to the right of Settings) enables you to look for content that is stored on the device. Search results also include a hierarchical view of the product family for the entered search term.

Navigation Bar

  • A: Visual Browser is the default navigation method.
  • B: Menu Browser is a no-nonsense, text-based menu with sliding sub-menus.
  • C: Curently Viewing jumps to the last content item you viewed.
