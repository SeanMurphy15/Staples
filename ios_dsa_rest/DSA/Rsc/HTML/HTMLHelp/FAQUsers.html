Frequently Asked Questions

Field Representatives/Users

Q: How many categories can I have in the DSA?
A: Theoretically, there is no limit to the number of categories or products in the application. However, categories can only be two levels deep.

Q: What is the purpose of checking in?
A: Checking In allows your administrator to track how the application is used, which attachments were opened and for how long, how much time is spent with each contact, etc.

Q: What information is stored on the iPad?
A: Your contacts, product categories, and content (images, documents, and videos) are stored on the iPad and automatically updated whenever an Internet connection is detected.

Q: What if an Internet connection is not available in the field?
A: Since the content from your Salesforce account is stored on the device, all features are available even when Internet connectivity is not.

Q: What are the system requirements for running DSA?
A: Click here to view the system requirements for DSA.

Q: Can I send any information to contacts from the application?
A: Images and documents (PowerPoint presentations, PDF files, Word documents, etc., can be forwarded to contacts via e-mail. Please note that a valid e-mail address must be set up on your iPad to use this feature.

Q: How does the rating system work?
A: The purpose of the ratings system is to allow field reps to provide feedback on various presentation materials used during sales meetings. When you go to check out, a list of materials you opened is listed and, along with other options, you can vote (in a scale of one to five) on the quality of each attachment you opened. For more information on this feature, click here.

Q: Do I need to check out?
A: You should check out whenever a meeting ends or you meet with a different contact. Checking out accomplishes the following:

  • Ends logging of the particular session.
  • Required to send any attachments you have flagged for e-mailing.
  • Allows you to use the feedback process.
