Entering Product Detail View

Selecting any of the Parent Categories (product families) will take you into the Product Detail View. The Detail View is where you can see the content related to the given category, product or product family.

Items within the Product Detail View

  • (A) The background image for this category as configured in Salesforce. If none was selected, the homescreen with appear.
  • (B) The title of the selected product, followed by a brief description (from Salesforce data).
  • (C) Related Content for this product, including brochures, white papers, images, videos, etc., as configured in Salesforce.
  • (D) The Product Gallery allows you to switch the products in the current category by swiping or tapping.
